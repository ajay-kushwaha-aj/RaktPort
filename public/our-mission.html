Core Values

The principles guiding every decision we make

01

Compassion First

Humanity is at the heart of every system we build. Every feature, every data field, every API endpoint exists to serve the donor, the patient, and the healthcare worker — not the platform.

02

Radical Transparency

Every request is traceable. Every RTID is verifiable. Every data access is logged. We build trust by making our processes visible, auditable, and open to scrutiny.

03

Technology with Purpose

We use modern technology — not for its own sake — but to reduce friction in life-or-death situations. Every technical decision is tested against this: does it save lives faster?

04

Integrity in Data

We adhere strictly to the DPDP Act 2023, IEC 62304 software lifecycle standards, and ISO 13485 quality principles — because the integrity of health data is non-negotiable.

Three Pillars of Our Vision

What a future powered by RaktPort looks like

Universal Connectivity

Every donor, hospital, and blood bank in India — connected in real time. Blood availability visible, searchable, and actionable across all 28+ states.

Zero-Compromise Safety

No forged records, no commercial blood trade, no data exploitation. RaktPort's security and compliance framework protects donors and patients with equal rigour.

Self-Sustaining Community

A voluntary donation culture so deeply embedded in communities that emergency blood shortages become a solvable — and eventually, a solved — problem.

ABDM Integration Roadmap

Embedding RaktPort into India's national digital health ecosystem

ABHA ID Linkage (Live) Voluntary linking of Ayushman Bharat Health Account IDs to donor profiles for national record interoperability
PHR Push (Roadmap) Push donation certificates to donors' ABHA-linked Personal Health Records via HIE-CM consent artefacts
HIU Integration (Roadmap) Enable NHA-registered hospitals to request blood-group and eligibility data as Health Information Users (HIUs)
National Blood Registry (Roadmap) Contribute anonymised donation trends to MoHFW's national blood supply intelligence framework

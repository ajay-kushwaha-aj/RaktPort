Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 8th Nov,2025
Last Updated: 8th Nov, 2025

At RaktPort, your privacy and trust are our top priorities. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information while using our website and mobile app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect data to improve service efficiency and donor–hospital connectivity.

  • Personal Info: Name, Age, Gender, Blood Group, Contact Details, Location.
  • Technical Info: Device type, IP address, usage logs.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

  • To match donors and hospitals efficiently.
  • To send alerts and improve user experience.
  • To enhance security and prevent misuse.

3. Data Sharing

We only share necessary data with verified hospitals/blood banks or when legally required. No user data is sold or rented.

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is encrypted and securely stored in compliant cloud servers. Access is limited to authorized personnel only.

5. Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent for data use at any time.

6. Cookies

We use cookies for analytics. You may disable them in your browser settings.

7. Children’s Privacy

Users below 18 years are not permitted to register. If detected, their data will be deleted immediately.

8. Data Retention

Data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill service purposes or legal obligations.

9. Updates

We may update this policy periodically. You’ll be notified of major changes via app or email.

Blood Donation Guidelines

Blood donation is a noble act that saves lives. These guidelines ensure safety and awareness for every donor contributing through RaktPort.

1. Donor Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: 18–65 years
  • Weight: Minimum 50 kg
  • Hemoglobin: At least 12.5 g/dL
  • Pulse: 60–100 bpm, regular
  • Blood Pressure: 100–140/60–90 mm Hg
  • Donation Gap: 3 months (men), 4 months (women)
  • Should be physically and mentally healthy

Do NOT donate blood if:

  • You have fever, cold, or infection.
  • You had surgery, vaccination, or major illness in the last 6 months.
  • You are pregnant or breastfeeding.
  • You consumed alcohol or tobacco in the last 24 hours.

2. Preparation Before Donation

  • Eat a light, balanced meal before donating.
  • Stay hydrated — drink water or juice.
  • Sleep well (6–8 hours) the night before.
  • Avoid smoking or alcohol 24 hours prior.
  • Carry a valid ID (Aadhaar, Voter ID, ABHA ID etc.).

3. During Donation

  • Process duration: 15–20 minutes.
  • Only sterile, single-use needles are used.
  • Blood collected: 350–450 ml.
  • Relax — trained medical staff ensure your comfort.

4. After Donation

  • Rest for 10–15 minutes.
  • Drink fluids and eat snacks provided.
  • Avoid strenuous activity for 24 hours.
  • Keep the bandage on for 3–4 hours.

5. Benefits of Blood Donation

  • Stimulates new blood cell formation.
  • Reduces risk of heart and liver diseases.
  • Provides free health screening.
  • Promotes well-being and community spirit.

6. RaktPort Safety Commitment

  • Data encryption and verified donor records.
  • No data shared without user consent.
  • 100% authenticated donor–hospital matching.

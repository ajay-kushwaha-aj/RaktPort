About RaktPort

RaktPort is a digital bridge that connects voluntary blood donors, hospitals, and blood banks through real-time verified requests and data transparency. Our mission is to ensure that safe blood reaches every patient who needs it — without delay, exploitation, or uncertainty.

What We Do

  • For Donors: Easy registration, donation tracking, and alerts for nearby needs.
  • For Hospitals: Verified donor matching and efficient patient request handling.
  • For Blood Banks: Real-time stock visibility and integration with hospitals.

Why Choose RaktPort?

We go beyond matching donors — we build trust. Each request and donor is authenticated through secure backend systems, preventing misuse and fraud. RaktPort operates on transparency, community participation, and digital safety.

Our Vision

To create India’s most reliable, secure, and accessible digital blood donation network — where no life is lost due to the unavailability of blood.

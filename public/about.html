Who We Are

RaktPort (from Sanskrit: "Rakta" = Blood, "Port" = Gateway) is a national-scale digital infrastructure for blood donation management — designed, built, and maintained in India.

We go beyond simple donor matching. Every request, every donor, and every unit of blood is authenticated through secure, auditable backend systems — preventing misuse, reducing fraud, and ensuring blood reaches those who truly need it.

Founded as a biomedical student initiative, RaktPort has grown into a production-grade platform aligning with India's digital health standards, including the Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABDM) and national cybersecurity frameworks.

Every donation is a verified, traceable, and dignified act of saving lives

What We Do

A complete ecosystem connecting every stakeholder in the blood donation journey

For Donors

Easy registration, eligibility tracking, live donation countdown, health tips, impact certificates, and emergency blood need alerts — empowering voluntary donors at every step.

For Hospitals

NACO/MoHFW-compliant blood requisition forms, real-time Firestore matching, urgency tiering (Emergency / Urgent / Routine), transfusion tracking, and QR-enabled RTID verification.

For Blood Banks

Real-time stock visibility, verified organisation dashboards, national blood inventory ledger, fraud alert system, and full audit log with RTID traceability.

RTID Tracking System

Every donation and requisition receives a Luhn-36 checksum-validated 19-character Tracking ID — enabling end-to-end traceability from donor to transfusion recipient.

Security & Privacy

AES-256 encryption, role-based access, MFA enforcement, penetration-tested infrastructure, and full DPDP Act 2023 compliance with a designated Grievance Officer.

PWA & Offline Ready

Installable Progressive Web App with service-worker caching — ensures donors can access critical information and submit emergency requests even in low-connectivity conditions.

Regulatory & Standards Awareness

Built from day one with India's healthcare regulatory framework in mind

IEC 62304 · Software Lifecycle

Software as a Medical Device

RaktPort's critical modules are assessed as Class B SaMD. Software development, validation, and problem resolution follow IEC 62304:2006+AMD1 lifecycle procedures.

DPDP Act 2023 · MANDATORY

India's Digital Data Protection

Full compliance with Data Principal rights, consent architecture, Grievance Officer appointment, CERT-In incident reporting, and data residency within India.

ABDM · Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission

ABHA & Health Record Integration

Aligned with HIE-CM consent framework. ABHA ID linkage, ABHA-based Health Information Exchange with time-bound consent artefacts, and NHA empanelment roadmap.

ISO 13485 · Quality Management

Medical Device QMS Alignment

Development processes aligned with ISO 13485:2016 — including design controls, document control, CAPA management, and internal quality audits.

Cybersecurity Framework

Penetration Testing & Encryption

Annual third-party VAPT, AES-256 at-rest encryption, TLS 1.3 in transit, ransomware-resilient backups, and CERT-In 6-hour incident reporting compliance.

ISO 14971 · Risk Management

Software Risk Assessment

All production changes undergo documented risk-benefit assessment. Risk files are maintained per ISO 14971:2019 with residual risk justification before each release.

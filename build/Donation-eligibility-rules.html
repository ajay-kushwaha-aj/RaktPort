Donation Eligibility Rules

Make sure you meet the requirements before donating blood

Age Requirement

18–65 years old

Weight Requirement

Minimum 50 kg (110 lbs)

Health Status

Good general health and no active infections

Donation Frequency

Every 3 months (men), every 4 months (women)

Temporary Restrictions

Recent tattoo or piercing (within 6 months)
Pregnant or breastfeeding
Recent surgery or major illness
Certain medications or antibiotics
Travel to malaria or high-risk areas
Chronic conditions (consult your doctor)

* Always consult with medical staff during screening to ensure you’re eligible to donate safely.
